TEAM


Here are the people currently working at Swift Interactive:

DENNIS LÖFGREN

CEO / DIRECTOR / LEAD DESIGNER

Dennis is a passionate game designer and graphic designer with one foot firmly placed in the Star Wars universe. With his unlimited pool of ideas, Swift Interactive will probably never run out of potential games to make.

LEO ALKBERG

LEAD PROGRAMMER

Leo is red/white aligned planeswalker who does gameplay programming for Swift Interactive. He likes to listen to experimental music and to play Magic: the Gathering. He was at one point ranked #10 in the world on Luigi Circuit time trials in Mario Kart Wii in 2008, something he brings up repeatedly.

LIRIDON IMERI

LEAD ARTIST

Liridon is an individual from the far away land of Skåne. This “danish” person is a 3D model/sculpt environment/prop artist, and he’s working on learning 2D concept drawing. He’s been playing World of Warcraft since 2005 and is still playing it. Duel him anytime… if you dare.

VINCENT SCHUBBERT

TECHNICAL ARTIST

Vincent or Vince, as some people call him, is a killer technical artist. He always gives the task 110%. Besides that he enjoys doing level design if the opportunity appears. They also call him The Headshot Machine, so don’t challenge this guy to a 1v1.

CARL STENGÅRD

LEVEL DESIGNER

Carl is a man who really enjoys involuntarily dissecting levels to an unhealthy degree when his passion flares up. Which does come in handy considering he’s the main level designer at Swift Interactive. As part game designer, he tries his hardest to leash Dennis’ ideas to a realistic level.

JOAKIM BÖRJESSON

PROGRAMMER

Joakim is a programmer who likes to get involved with more projects than he should. As with many others on the team, he also really enjoys level design. In his spare time which he doesn’t have, he is not addicted to Old School RuneScape and one day, he will make a game with very specific camera angles.

JESSICA MATTILA

ARTIST / ANIMATOR

Jessica is someone who likes creating characters, whether it is for games she’ll never make or DND campaigns she’ll never play. She can’t stop. Someone help her.
