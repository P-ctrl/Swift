Here you can find open positions at Swift Interactive or positions that will potentially be interesting to us in the near future:
JOBS
TECH ART INTERN
We’re looking for a Technical Artist Intern. You need to have an interest in technical art and visual scripting.

Your job would be to do tech art in Unity for our game but also to learn about the field and about practical game development at a game studio.
SOUND DESIGNER
We’re looking for a Sound Designer with experience or knowledge making sounds for video games or similar fields.

Your job would be to create and implement sound effects into our game which is being developed in Unity. If you want some kind of third party software, that’s a conversation we’re open to.
STORY DESIGNER
We’re looking for a Story Designer that has experience or knowledge of story writing & cinematography.

Your task would be to oversee the story of the game. Writing dialogue, directing cutscenes and making sure the whole story is impactful and makes sense. You would also be leading the casting process.

As the majority of our cast of characters is female, we’re looking for a woman to fill this role.
CFO / COO
We’re looking for a Chief Financial and Operational Officer with experience or knowledge of running a business. If you have experience running a game studio, that’s a plus.

We’re looking for someone that can run the company day to day so our CEO can put most of his time on the game.

If you have game industry experience and can double as a producer, that’s also a big plus.
ANIMATOR
We’re looking for a 3D animator with about 5 years of game studio development experience and has at least one released game.

As we’re a new studio, we really want to hire some people with experience of game development past the prototype stage.

You need to have a good understanding of the entire 3D character animation pipeline, including skinning and rigging.
PROGRAMMER
We’re looking for a programmer with about 5 years of game studio development experience and has at least one released game.

As we’re a new studio, we really want to hire some people with experience of game development past the prototype stage.

You need to know C# and have experience with 3D game coding in Unity.
